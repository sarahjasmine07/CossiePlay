Project Plan

Goals

Cossie Play is a simple and creative website to attract audience who are interested in fancy dress costumes and cosplay parties. The purpose of Cossie Play website is to expand their small local business to mass media as this generation is more influenced in the digital world. This allows the participants to find information regarding Cossie Play more easily as it is also less time consuming. Moreover, the development of a website is known to have many advantages such as free advertising, larger broad reach of audience, and has a smaller budget cost compared to other options. Therefore, Cossie Play plans to promote and redefine their brand image.

Success Evaluation

Cossie Play is a small local business which prioritize the importance of their audience needs and wants. The website offers the insight information about Cossie Play and how the customers could take advantage of their promotions and bookings. Moreover, a range of costumes is offered to customers which enable them to be more creative and socially acceptable with other people. Moreover, the layout of the website is simple and appealing.
